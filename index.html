Hi! I'm Philip, a Concept Designer

About Me

A recent graduate from UCR, with a BA in Art (Studio); my main focus in art is “creature and character concept design”. My works consist of character illustrations and concepts from a variety of different media, usually combined to create a new concept. Main program I use is Procreate and Clip Studio Paint, but can also work with a wide range of mediums, including painting, sculpture, photography, and video. I am someone who wishes to continuously learn and grow in their craft while also listening to good stories and helping those who need a spark of motivation toward their goal.
